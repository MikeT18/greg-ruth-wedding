By Car

There is ample free parking available at Ribby Hall Village. The venue is easily accessible from the M55 motorway:

  • From Manchester/South: M6 North to M55 West, Exit at Junction 3, follow signs to Kirkham then Wrea Green
  • From Preston/North: M6 South to M55 West, Exit at Junction 3
  • From Blackpool: Take the A583 towards Preston, turn onto B5259 to Wrea Green

By Train

Nearest Train Station: Kirkham & Wesham

Distance from venue: 3 miles (10 minutes by taxi)

Connections: Direct trains from Preston, Blackpool, Manchester, and Liverpool

Taxis are usually available at the station, or you can pre-book one of the taxi companies listed above.

Airport Connections

Manchester Airport

Distance: 45 miles (approximately 1 hour drive)

Train to Preston, then taxi to venue (~90 minutes total)

Liverpool John Lennon Airport

Distance: 50 miles (approximately 1 hour 15 minutes drive)

Train to Preston, then taxi to venue (~2 hours total)

Group Travel & Coaches

If you're traveling as a group and would like to arrange a shared minibus or coach, please contact us and we can help coordinate group bookings.
